About Dojran

Lake, history, and places to visit.

Lake Dojran

Known for its traditional fishing and calm atmosphere. Great for lakeside walks and sunsets.

Old & New Dojran

Historic core and modern tourist infrastructure, making it ideal for weekend trips and longer stays.

Culture & history

The town suffered heavy destruction in WWI but has since rebuilt its tourist role in the region.

What to visit

Promenade, local museums, thermal sources, and traditional restaurants are among the highlights.
